MARY SLESSOR


Mary Slessor — a woman whose courage, compassion, and unwavering dedication left an indelible mark on our nation's history. At a time when fear and superstition endangered the lives of innocent children, she stood boldly for justice, challenging harmful traditions and advocating for the voiceless.
Slessor did not just preach change; she lived among us, learned our languages, respected our cultures, and earned our trust. In Calabar and beyond, she became more than a missionary — she became a mother, a judge, a teacher, and a beacon of hope. Her legacy lives on in the values of compassion, equality, and education that continue to shape communities today. We remember her not only as a Scottish woman but as one of our own — a true Nigerian in spirit and heart.
Her legacy continue to inspire generations to come!
